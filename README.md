# Highlighter — Notes & AI Summary

A modern, lightweight Chrome Extension to highlight text, add tagged notes, and manage your research across any webpage.

## Features
- 🖍️ **Effortless Highlighting**: Select text on any webpage to reveal a floating toolbar and highlight using customizable colors.
- 📝 **Notes & Tags**: Attach detailed thoughts and searchable tags to your highlights through a quick popup.
- 🗂️ **Sidebar Organization**: Open the side panel to view, search, and filter your highlights. Switch between "Current Page" context and "All Pages" globally.
- 🌗 **Dark & Light Themes**: Built-in modes natively integrated with your reading setup.
- 📤 **Export & Share**: Easily export your curated highlights and notes to a Markdown (`.md`) file or just copy them directly to your clipboard.

## How to Install

This extension runs locally. To install it in Chrome:

1. **Open Extensions Page**: Type `chrome://extensions/` into your Chrome address bar and hit Enter.
2. **Enable Developer Mode**: Toggle on the "Developer mode" switch located in the top right corner.
3. **Load Unpacked**: Click the "Load unpacked" button in the top left corner.
4. **Select Folder**: Choose the `Chrome_Extention` folder containing this repository on your computer.
5. **Pin the Extension (Optional)**: Click the puzzle piece icon next to your Chrome profile picture and click the pin icon next to "Highlighter" for quick access.

## How to Use

1. **Highlight Text**: Simply select any text on a webpage. A tiny floating toolbar will pop up. 
2. **Choose Color**: Click the pencil icon or any color swatch to establish your highlight.
3. **Add Context**: Hover over your newly created highlight, then click to add a note or attach tags via the popup window.
4. **Manage Everything**: Click the Highlighter extension icon in your Chrome toolbar to bring up the side panel. From here, you can search phrases, filter by tags, format settings, and export your work.
